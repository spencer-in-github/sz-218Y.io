2 Housing burden

Housing cost burden is evaluated by the percentage of housing costs on an individual’s total income. A housing cost over 30% of one’s income is considered as burden, where >50% is considered as severe burden. ## Renter-occupied housing burden Under such definition, 11,440 out of the total 20,224 households in East Palo Alto are burdened with housing costs. Among them, 29% is severely burdened, and 27% is moderately burdened.
